PS4 VR

What is PS4 VR


PS4 Vr is a virtual reality pack you can buy from local stores. To play with this VR you need some type of Playstation console. The regular VR cost around $300 and comes with controllers. The VR's can even play movies and shows of your liking. Playstation recently made a cinimatic mode which makes it seem as if you're in a movie theater. With a nice set of headphones and cinematic mode set on medium, it provides a great experience with one caveat. Although, it can get uncomfortable to wear that headset after a couple of hours.
